Color Buckets

A game by Eric Douglas, Tim Alander, Denzel Rice, and Rohan Natraj

Instructions

Goal: Replenish the secondary color buckets before they run out by combining primary colors in the multi-colored mixing buckets.

  • Connect buckets by clicking on them in sequence.
  • Primary color buckets can be disconnected by double clicking on them.
  • Primary color buckets can only be connected to two different mixing buckets at a time.
  • Secndary color buckets will flash when they get low on supplies! Watch out — if they get too low it's game over.
